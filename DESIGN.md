# Design System: Resuman Refined

**Project ID:** 863361798468670855

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The aesthetic is "Editorial Premium." It balances high-end minimalism with warm, approachable earth tones. It feels airy, uncluttered, and highly trustworthy, evoking the feel of a premium consulting agency or a high-end editorial publication.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Parchment Cream (#F7F4EF)**: Base background color. Extremely light warm grey/cream. Used for full page backgrounds to avoid harsh pure whites.
* **Espresso Black (#2C2318)**: Primary text and high-emphasis elements. A rich, very dark brown that acts as a softer, more elegant black.
* **Warm Taupe (#C4B5A0)**: Secondary structural color. Used for borders, subtle dividers, and inactive states.
* **Sage Green (#7B9B7E)**: Positive semantic color. Used for high scores, success states, and subtle highlights.
* **Clay Red (#A85C4A)**: Negative semantic color. Used for low scores and critical warnings.
* **Olive Accent (#4A4535)**: Primary interactive color. Used for main CTA buttons and active focus states.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Serif Display (Cormorant Garamond)**: Used exclusively for large headers (H1, H2, brand wordmarks). Gives the editorial, premium feel. Weights: 400 (regular) for massive text, 600 (semibold) for emphasis.
* **Sans-Serif Utility (Inter)**: Used for body copy, labels, and small UI elements. Provides high legibility and modern contrast. Weights: 400 for body, 500 (medium) for buttons and labels. Tracking (letter-spacing) is slightly increased on uppercase labels.

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Pill-shaped (fully rounded). Primary buttons use Olive Accent with light text and a subtle drop shadow on hover. Secondary buttons are transparent with a 1px border.
* **Cards/Containers:** Generously rounded corners (2rem). Backgrounds use a slightly transparent cream with background blur (glassmorphism) and a whisper-soft drop shadow to create depth without heaviness.
* **Inputs/Forms:** Generous padding. Soft 1rem border radius. Background is a slightly darker, transparent parchment with a 1px border that shifts to Olive on focus.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Whitespace:** Airy and expansive. Sections are separated by massive vertical padding (5rem to 8rem). Elements within cards have comfortable breathing room.
* **Alignment:** Centered hero sections with staggered, masonry-style or spacious grid layouts for dashboards.
* **Depth:** Flat by default, with depth introduced only via subtle blur effects and soft shadows on elevated cards.
